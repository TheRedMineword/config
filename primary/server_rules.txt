In addition, we impose the following rules and guidelines on members of this server:

Violation of these rules will result in disciplinary action

Follow the rules of Netiquette and Online Safety
- **§4)**
Advertising websites and other servers without permission is prohibited. **We cannot moderate the content contained there and are not responsible for it!!**
-# Invites and links in bio and status are included

**== Our Community Guidelines ==**
In order to maintain a safe environmet for all our members, our guidelines are as follows:
- **§1)**
Maintain a polite attitude (being silly is fine, until it crosses into disrespect).
- **§2)**
Don't spam messages/pings (especially `@everyone`, `@member`, `@ally`).
- **§3)**
Don't use mass pings (for example: `@everyone`, `@member`) UNLESS you have been given explicit permission from an officer or above, or it is absolutely necessary (it's recommended to get a First Officer to do this).
- **§4)**
Do not partake in any kind of harmful/illegal activities on our server. If you see such activities occuring, immediately inform a First Officer.
- **§5)**
Do not use the server in order to advertise services (like artwork)(this is due to a Discord exploit that could use this as a vector of attack).
- **§6)**
Do not leak/expose/distribute any White Star (WS) plans UNLESS your First Officer allows you.
- **§7)**
Use channels for their intended purpose!
